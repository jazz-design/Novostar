Frequently Asked Questions?

What is a Direct Immigration Services?

Direct Immigration Services refer to the process of applying for immigration or a visa without intermediaries or third-party agents. It involves working directly with government immigration offices or official consultants to ensure a transparent and efficient application process. This approach can save time and costs while ensuring compliance with legal requirements.

What are the Benefits of Visa Consultancy?

Visa consultancy services provide expert guidance throughout the application process, increasing the chances of approval. Key benefits include:

  • Expert Advice – Get professional insights on visa eligibility and requirements.

  • Error-Free Application – Avoid common mistakes that lead to rejection.

  • Faster Processing – Consultants streamline the process, reducing delays.

  • Legal Compliance – Ensure adherence to immigration laws and policies.

What We should do If our Visa get Rejection?

If your visa application is rejected, don’t panic. Follow these steps:

  • Find the Reason – Check the rejection letter for details.

  • Consult an Expert – Get professional advice to fix issues.

  • Reapply Smartly – Correct mistakes and strengthen your case.

  • Appeal if Allowed – Some visa types offer an appeal process.

  • Explore Alternatives – Consider other visa categories.

Which Documents are Required to Apply Visa Online?

The required documents may vary depending on the country and visa type, but generally, you’ll need:

  • Valid Passport – Minimum 6 months of validity.

  • Visa Application Form – Filled and submitted online.

  • Photos – Passport-sized, as per specifications.

  • Financial Proof – Bank statements or sponsorship letter.

  • Travel Itinerary – Flight & hotel bookings.

  • Supporting Documents – Work permit, invitation letter, or study enrollment.
